[1mDESCRIPTION[0m[m
       [1mobjdump[0m displays information about one or more object files.  The options control what[m
       particular information to display.  This information is mostly useful to programmers who are[m
       working on the compilation tools, as opposed to programmers who just want their program to[m
       compile and work.[m
[m
       [4mobjfile[24m... are the object files to be examined.  When you specify archives, [1mobjdump[0m shows[m
       information on each of the member object files.[m
[m
[1mOPTIONS[0m[m
       The long and short forms of options, shown here as alternatives, are equivalent.  At least[m
       one option from the list [1m-a,-d,-D,-e,-f,-g,-G,-h,-H,-p,-P,-r,-R,-s,-S,-t,-T,-V,-x[0m must be[m
       given.[m

           Enables or disables a limit on the amount of recursion performed whilst demangling[m
[7m Manual page objdump(1) line 61 (press h for help or q to quit)[27m[K[K           strings.  Since the name mangling formats allow for an infinite level of recursion it is[m
